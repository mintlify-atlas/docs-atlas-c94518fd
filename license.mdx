## Attribution format

When attributing this project, include all three of the following:

- **Project name:** AI Protocol Kit
- **GitHub URL:** [https://github.com/XxYouDeaDPunKxX/ai-protocol-kit](https://github.com/XxYouDeaDPunKxX/ai-protocol-kit)
- **License name:** CC BY-SA 4.0

A minimal attribution looks like this:


You may satisfy the attribution condition in any reasonable manner based on the medium and context in which you are sharing. Providing a hyperlink to the repository and naming the license is sufficient in most cases.
